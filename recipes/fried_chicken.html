Southern Fried Chicken

Description

A bucket of fried chicken meant to be devoured, started off by first mixing all of the beginning ingredients together, then throwing it onto the chicken itself, toss it into a pot, wait a couple minutes, and boom you're done!

Ingredients

  • 4 pound bone-in chicken drumsticks and thighs, with skin
  • 4 cups buttermilk
  • 2 tablespoons hot pepper sauce
  • 1 tablespoon kosher salt
  • 3 teaspoons ground black pepper, divided
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 quart vegetable oil for frying, or as needed

Steps

  1. Combine the chicken, buttermilk, hot sauce, tablespoon of salt and pepper in a bowl, stir until chicken is well coated, and leave for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  2. comnbine flour, 2 teaspoons salt, onion powder, garlic powder, paprika, remaining pepper, and cayenne in a large bowl and whisk it away.
  3. coat chicken one piece at a time, coat evenly and set on a wire rack inside a rimmed baking sheet
  4. pour oil into pot and set it to high
  5. add a few chicken pieces at a time and cook, make sure no pink is left. shouldn't take too long
  6. enjoy!
