Pizza Dough

Description

Stir and mix all the beginning ingredients in a bowl, toss it into a preheated oven, wait 15, turn it, wait 15, bing bong, you've got pizza(dough)!

  • 1/4 cup whole weat flour
  • 3 cups all-prupose flour>
  • 1/4 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1 1/4 teaspoons salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • all-purpose flour, for dusting
