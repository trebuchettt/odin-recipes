Chicken Enchiladas

Description

Tasty and flavorful, spicy and exciting. We got it all here baby! With our creamy chicken enchiladas nothing can go wrong!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 lbs skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 12 (6 inch) flour tortillas
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 medium poblano pepper, seeded and chopped
  • 1 small yellow bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 1 (10 ounce) can dieced tomatoes and green chiles
  • 1/2 *7 ounce) package cream cheese
  • 4 ounces sour cream
  • 4 cups shredded cheddar-monterey jack cheese blend, divided
  • 1 cup refried beans
  • 1 (15 ounce) can red enchilada sauce

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 325 degrees
  2. place chicken onto large foil sheet and season with 1 tablespoon salt, 1/2 chili powder, and 1/2 teaspoon cumin.place chicken on a rimmed baking sheet
  3. bake in a preheated oven until tender and no pink, shred it set it aside to continue cook
  4. prepare the tortillas, spread butter and heat a skillet on medium heat, toast each side by 10 seconds and set aside
  5. heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. add onion, poblano ppper, pepper and cook stri every 2 minutes, add al lthe other vegetables and then the cheese and stir it all until tender
  6. spread it onto the tortilla, fry the beans, spread them, pour any sauce and cheese onto, then roll it
  7. bake it for aboutr 30 minutes and serve.
